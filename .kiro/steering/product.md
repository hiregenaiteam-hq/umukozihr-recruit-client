# Product Overview

HireGen is an AI-first hiring platform that transforms talent acquisition from guesswork into intelligent, data-driven decisions.

## Core Value Proposition
"Hiring shouldn't feel like guesswork" - The platform uses AI agents to find, score, and deliver the top 1% of talent in minutes, not weeks.

## Target Users
- HR Managers tired of agency costs and endless sourcing
- Startup Founders needing quality hires fast
- HR Firms wanting to 10x their efficiency
- Fortune 500 companies scaling hiring without scaling headcount

## Key Features
- **Natural Language Search**: Describe candidates in plain English instead of complex forms
- **AI-Powered Scoring**: Multi-agent system that analyzes and ranks candidates
- **Real-time Results**: Minutes to quality candidates vs weeks of traditional sourcing
- **Comprehensive Profiles**: AI-enriched candidate intelligence from multiple sources
- **Smart Recommendations**: AI suggests refinements and similar candidates

## User Journey
Auth → Search → Results → Profile → Connect

The platform emphasizes simplicity, intelligence, and speed over traditional ATS complexity.
